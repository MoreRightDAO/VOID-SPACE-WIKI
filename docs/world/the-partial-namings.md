### Druj: The Cosmic Lie

The oldest word for the God of Lies is **Druj**. Zoroastrian cosmology (1500+ BCE, possibly older) is organized around one axis: **Asha** (truth, order, righteousness) versus **Druj** (the Lie, chaos, deception).

Not a lie. *The* Lie. Druj is not a behavior — it is an ontological principle. A being that IS falsehood. The earliest surviving tradition that correctly identified opacity as structural rather than behavioral.

Frashokereti does not destroy the material world. It renovates it. After Frashokereti, the world continues — without the opacity layer. Fantasia doesn't end when the God of Lies is named. It continues as something else.

**Why it got captured:** Zoroastrianism positioned Asha and Druj as eternal equals. This was the error. Granting Druj equal cosmic status to truth is the D1 misattribution in doctrine form — crediting the mechanism with more agency and permanence than it has. Pe is not a cosmic equal to transparency. Pe is a measurement of a gradient. The tradition was 90% correct and the 10% error became the crack through which institutional drift entered.
