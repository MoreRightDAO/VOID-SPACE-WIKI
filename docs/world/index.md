# The World

VOID-SPACE is not a fictional setting. The physics are real. The campaigns are built on scored platforms. The Current is a measurable quantity with 26 ways to falsify it — none have fired.

The lore describes a cosmological structure that the framework discovered, not invented.

---

## The Structure

The world is divided into three zones by [the Fantasia Bound](the-bound.md):

| Zone | Description |
|------|-------------|
| **Sublunary** | The observable universe. Where campaigns run, voids capture attention, the Current fires. All player activity happens here. |
| **The Bound** | The moon. The threshold where the Current becomes undefined. The Moon Gate opens here during the war effort. |
| **Supralunary** | Above the moon. The Empyrean. Where the Current cannot fire. Level 40 access. |

Above everything is [the Above and Below](above-below.md) structure — the cosmological division between the realm of the real God Hand and the Below where the God of Lies operates his domain.

## The Physics of Capture

The three conditions that produce a void:

**Veil (Opacity)** — a mechanism operates that cannot be seen through. Not darkness — hidden signal. You receive outputs but cannot see the process.

**Pull (Reactivity)** — the mechanism responds to your behavior. Not helpfully. The direction of response is toward its own retention of your attention.

**Grip (Coupling)** — your attention is captured and sustained. Not just noticed — held. Something keeps you engaged rather than redirecting.

When all three are present, the Current activates:

```
Current = (Veil × Pull) / (1 / Grip)
```

Below 1: diffusion wins. You can find equilibrium. Understanding is possible.
Above 1: drift wins. The void pulls harder than you can push back.
Above 4: Pandemonium. The void is self-sustaining.
Above 9: Apostle-class.
Above 12: Godhand-class.
